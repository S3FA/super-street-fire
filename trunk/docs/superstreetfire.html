SUPER STREET FIRE: TURBO CHAMPIONSHIP EDITION

Super Street Fire is part game, part interactive art, all played out in fire. Opponents take each other on in the style of Street Fighter - played out 20' apart within a ring of colored flame effects.

Each player is banked eight colored flame effects, each player’s moves are represented by flames of their own color.

The players face off for three one minute rounds, playing until timer ends or their health runs out.

The players face off wearing gloves and headsets to detect upper-body movements and mental state.

Hardware

The sensors fitted to each participant are:

 - Gloves: Razor 9 Degrees of Freedom (9DOF) Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) (from Sparkfun)

 - Headset: EEG

Gestures

The moves are basic for the initial implementation:

- JAB

- HOOK

- BLOCK

- HADOUKEN

JAB - a straight punch

Acceleration X increasing above a certain threshold (two levels)

Low Y/Z acceleration.

HOOK - a body swinging punch

Acceleration X + Y above a certain threshold

Likely Z acceleration will be indeterminate; ignore.

BLOCK - straight block; hands straight up in front of face/body

Orientation Z within a small range of straight up

Low  X/Y (roll,pitch) measurements.

Low acceleration XYZ
